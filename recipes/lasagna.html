Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for lasagna. The ingredients include pasta, sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta and mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the pan by laying out pasta sheets on the bottom.
  2. Layer tomato sauce and cheese between the pasta sheets.
  3. Bake the lasagna at 400 degrees F for one hour.
